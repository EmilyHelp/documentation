Blocklist Words Documentation

Command Description Usage
/blocklist Shows the list of blocklisted words or sentences. /blocklist
/blocklist word or /blocklist "sentence" Adds a word or sentence (in quotes) to the blocklist. /blocklist badword or /blocklist "no spamming allowed"
/removeb Removes a word or sentence from the blocklist. /removeb badword or /removeb "no spamming allowed"
/punishb Sets punishment for using blocklisted words (kick, mute, tmute, ban). Default: delete message. /punishb mute
