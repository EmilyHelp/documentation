Protect from Raid Documentation

Command Description Usage
/raidban Automatically bans new members to prevent raids. /raidban
/raidmute Automatically mutes all new members to prevent raids. /raidmute
/raidkick Automatically kicks all new members to prevent raids. /raidkick
/raidtmute Automatically mutes all new members for 1 hour to prevent raids. /raidtmute
/disableraid Disables all anti-raid measures (ban, mute, kick, tmute). /disableraid
