Whitelist Words Documentation

Command Description Usage
/addwhitelist Adds a word, link, contact, or other item to the whitelist, preventing bot deletion. /addwhitelist google.com or /addwhitelist hello
/whitelist Lists all whitelisted words, links, or items. /whitelist
/removew Removes a word or item from the whitelist. /removew google.com
/punishw Sets punishment for non-whitelisted content (kick, mute, tmute, ban). Default: delete message. /punishw ban
